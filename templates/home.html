Smart Grid Anomalies Detection

What is Smart Grids?

Smart grids are electricity network that use digital technologies, sensors and software to better match the supply and demand of electricity in real time while minimizing costs and maintaining the stability and reliability of the grid.

Why do we need Anomalies Detection app?

Smart Grids are vulnerable. Hackers can try to alter or steal data. They can attack the smart grid and it will result in transmission of incorrect data to the base station. Traditional security measures, such as statistical and signature-based detection techniques, have been introduced to mitigate these risks. However, these techniques fail to detect new or previously unseen attacks. Thatâ€™s why we are using AI to detect Smart Grids attacks.

How to use this app?

- Go to page Dataset to review the training dataset.

- Go to page Training to train the model using one of 3 ML algorithms: AutoEncoder-GAN, CNN and LSTM. You can also review the metrics there such as accuracy, true positive rate, false positive rate and f1 score.

- Go to page Detection to find and classify anomalies in your dataset.
